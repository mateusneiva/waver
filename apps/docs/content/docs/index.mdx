**Waver** is a premium Discord music bot designed to deliver crystal-clear, lag-free audio playback across all your favorite platforms — Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, and more.

Whether you're hosting a late-night listening party or just vibing solo, Waver makes it effortless.

## Why Waver?

- **High-Fidelity Audio**: Enjoy music the way it was meant to be heard, with no quality loss or stuttering.
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Play from Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, and direct links — all in one bot.
- **Queue Management**: Full control over your listening experience with a powerful queue system.
- **Low Latency**: Built for performance. Waver connects and plays faster than the competition.
- **Dead Simple**: No complicated setup. Invite, join a voice channel, and type `p!play`.

## Default Prefix

Waver uses the prefix **`p!`** for all commands. Simply type the prefix followed by the command name in any text channel the bot can access.

```bash
p!play your favorite song
```
